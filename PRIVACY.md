# Vivid Privacy Policy

## Overview
Vivid respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
- **Local Storage**: Knowledge items are stored locally on your device using SQLite
- **Authentication**: If using Privy auth, standard authentication data is processed
- **AI Processing**: Text sent to AI services (Groq/Gemini) for insights and chat

## Data Storage
- All notes and captures stored locally on your device
- Optional cloud sync with Supabase (encrypted)
- SecureStore used for sensitive data (tokens, keys)

## Third-Party Services
- **Privy**: Authentication services
- **Groq/Gemini**: AI processing (text only, no personal identifiers)
- **Supabase**: Optional cloud sync

## Your Rights
- Delete local data anytime via Settings
- Export your data as PDF
- No data shared with advertisers

## Contact
For privacy questions: privacy@vivid.app
