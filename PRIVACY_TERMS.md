# Privacy Policy for Vivid

**Last Updated:** May 1, 2026

## Introduction

Vivid ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** When you register using Privy authentication, we may collect your email address, name, and profile information.
- **Knowlege Content:** All notes, voice memos, images, PDFs, and links you capture are stored securely.
- **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you use the App to improve our services.

### Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, and app features used.
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** For authentication and preferences.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App's AI-powered features
- Process and organize your knowledge items using AI tagging and categorization
- Enable AI chat functionality with your knowledge base (RAG)
- Send you daily AI insights and reminders (with your consent)
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

## AI and Data Processing

### How We Use AI
- **Groq/Gemini/OpenAI:** We use third-party AI services to:
  - Auto-tag and categorize your knowledge items
  - Generate chat responses using your knowledge base (RAG)
  - Provide daily insights and summaries
- **Data sent to AI:** Only the content you capture (notes, voice transcriptions, etc.) is processed by AI services.
- **No selling of data:** We never sell your personal information or knowledge content to third parties.

### Data Storage
- **Supabase:** Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on Supabase.io)
- **Expo SQLite:** Local copy on your device for offline access
- **Sync:** Data syncs between your device and cloud when online

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your knowledge content with other users
- Use your data for advertising purposes

We MAY share information:
- With service providers (Supabase, Privy, AI services) to operate the App
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Authentication:** Secure authentication via Privy
- **Access Controls:** Strict access controls on our systems
- **Regular Security Reviews:** We regularly review our security practices

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability:** Export your knowledge items (PDF, JSON, etc.)
- **Opt-out:** Disable AI features or notifications

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
- **Email:** privacy@vivid.app
- **Through the App:** Settings > About > Contact Us
