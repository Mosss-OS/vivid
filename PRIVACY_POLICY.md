# Privacy Policy for Vivid

**Effective Date:** May 5, 2026

## Introduction
Vivid ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: If you create an account via Privy, we may collect email, social media handles, or wallet addresses
- **Knowledge Content**: All notes, voice memos, photos, PDFs, and links you capture are stored **locally on your device**
- **Profile Data**: Name and bio (stored locally)

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: We use anonymous analytics to improve the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version (for compatibility)

### AI Processing
When you use AI features (chat, insights, auto-tagging):
- Text content is sent to AI providers (Groq/Gemini) for processing
- **No personal identifiers** are attached to AI requests
- Content is **not stored** by AI providers for model training

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide and maintain the app
- Process AI requests (text only, no identifiers)
- Sync data if you enable cloud backup (optional)
- Improve app performance and features

## Data Storage & Security

### Local-First Architecture
- **Primary Storage**: All knowledge items stored locally on your device using SQLite
- **SecureStore**: Sensitive data (tokens, keys) stored using Expo SecureStore
- **No Cloud Required**: App works fully offline

### Optional Cloud Sync
- If enabled, data syncs with Supabase using encryption
- You can disable sync anytime in Settings
- You can delete all data from your device

## Third-Party Services
- **Privy**: Authentication services (see Privy's privacy policy)
- **Groq/Gemini**: AI processing (text only, no storage)
- **Supabase**: Optional cloud sync (encrypted)

## Your Rights & Choices
- **Delete Data**: Use "Clear Data" in Settings to remove all local data
- **Export Data**: Export your knowledge as PDF anytime
- **Disable AI**: Turn off AI features in Settings
- **Opt-Out**: Uninstall the app to stop all data collection

## Children's Privacy
Vivid is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated effective date.
