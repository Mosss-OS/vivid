## Description (4000 chars max)

**Capture Anything. Understand Everything.**

Vivid is your AI-powered second brain that transforms how you capture, organize, and interact with knowledge.

**CAPTURE ANYTHING**
• Text notes, voice memos, photos, PDFs, and web links
• AI auto-tagging and smart categorization
• Works offline, syncs when online

**ASK VIVID ANYTHING**
• Chat with your knowledge base using natural language
• Get AI-generated insights and connections
• Extract tasks and action items automatically

**DAILY AI INSIGHTS**
• Start each day with fresh insights from your knowledge
• Discover patterns and connections you missed
• Track your learning streak and momentum

**SMART ORGANIZATION**
• Auto-organized sections: All Notes, Ideas, Tasks, Projects
• Graph view shows connections between concepts
• Powerful search understands what you mean

**BEAUTIFUL & CALM**
• Minimalist design with soft dark/light modes
• Smooth animations and glassmorphism effects
• Micro-interactions with haptic feedback

**PRIVACY-FIRST**
• Local-first with end-to-end encryption
• Your data stays on your device
• Optional secure cloud sync

Download Vivid today and amplify your knowledge.
